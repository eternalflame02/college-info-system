The Reboot Kerala Hackathon

The Reboot Kerala Hackathon was conducted during the months of February and March, 2020 at various centers across Kerala.It is an initiative of Department of Higher Education – Government of Kerala and the Additional Skill Acquisition Program (ASAP) which aims to provide students of Higher Education Institutions a platform to solve some of the pressing problems we face in our daily lives, and thus inculcate a culture of product innovation and a mindset of problem-solving. Reboot Kerala Hackathon 2020 aims to bring students to the mainstream governance by receiving their solutions for the issues faced by various Government departments.
